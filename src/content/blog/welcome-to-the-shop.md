Welcome to Eclectic Boards & Cards — a home for the tabletop side of my brain.

Here's what you'll find as this place fills out:

- **Blog posts** — session thoughts, opinions, and whatever's on the table this week.
- **Reviews** — the long-haul kind, written after a game has survived (or not survived) real shelf time.
- **My games** — designs of my own, with print & play files you can download and try.

The look of the site is borrowed from an antique porcelain bottle stopper — the Enterprise Brewing Co. of San Francisco pressed their mark into it a century ago, and it still holds up. That's the bar for everything here: things worth keeping.

If you want new posts and print & play drops by email, there's a signup at the bottom of every page. No spam, ever.
