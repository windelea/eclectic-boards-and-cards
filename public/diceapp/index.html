      How to use

      • Add dice — tap a die in the bottom tray. d? makes a die with any number of sides.
      • ROLL — rolls every unlocked die on the table.
      • Tap a die — rerolls just that one.
      • Drag dice — arrange them however you like. Dice sitting near each other form a group and show their own subtotal.
      • Hold a die — shows what kind it is, plus options: Lock it so rolls skip it (padlock badge), change its color, duplicate it, or remove it.
      • Totals — the grand total lives in the top bar. Turn totals, subtotals, and history on or off in settings (⚙).
      • History — the clock button lists your recent rolls. The last three have a Restore button that takes the whole table back to that roll (tap twice to confirm).
      • Presets — in settings, save the current table under a name and reload it any time.
      • Install — add this page to your home screen and it works like an app, even offline:
        • iPhone / iPad — open this page in Safari, tap the Share button (the square with an arrow, in the bar at the bottom), scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add. (Other browsers on iPhone can't install apps — it has to be Safari.)
        • Android — open this page in Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu (top right), then tap Add to Home screen (on some phones it says Install app) and confirm. If Chrome offers an "Install" banner at the bottom of the screen, that works too.
